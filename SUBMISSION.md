## 📝 Project Overview
ResuNova is a premium AI-powered interview preparation platform. It uses Google Gemini 1.5 Flash to perform a deep "agentic analysis" of a candidate's resume against a specific job description. It generates a hyper-tailored 7-day preparation roadmap, technical/behavioral questions, and identifies bridgeable skill gaps to help candidates become "Placement Ready."

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React 19, Vite, Framer Motion, SCSS (Glassmorphism UI).
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js (v5), Multer, PDF-Parse.
- **AI Intelligence:** Google GenAI (Gemini 1.5 Flash), Zod (Schema Validation).
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas (Mongoose ODM).
- **Authentication:** JWT (HTTP-Only Cookies), Bcryptjs.

---

## 🔥 Key Features
1. **Agentic Resume Parsing:** Decodes expertise density and impact metrics from PDF resumes.
2. **Alignment Index scoring:** Calculates a real-time match score between profile and job requirements.
3. **7-Day Study Roadmap:** A deterministic daily timeline tailored for the specific interview.
4. **Precision Q&A:** AI-generated technical and behavioral questions based on the candidate's unique background.
5. **Skill Gap Mapping:** Visual identification of missing competencies with severity color-coding.
